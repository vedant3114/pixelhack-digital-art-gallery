Artist Biography

The Creative Journey

Ava Sharma began her artistic exploration in the digital realm, blending traditional artistic principles with cutting-edge technologies. Her work explores the intersection of human creativity and algorithmic processes.

With a background in both fine arts and computer science, Ava creates works that challenge our perceptions of beauty and reality. Her pieces often reflect on themes of consciousness, digital identity, and the future of human expression.

Technique & Style

Known for her vibrant color palettes and dynamic compositions, Ava's technique combines procedural generation with manual intervention. She uses proprietary algorithms to generate patterns and then adds her personal touch to create unique visual experiences.

Her art has been described as "contemporary impressionism meets digital surrealism" - capturing fleeting moments and emotions in a format that exists between the physical and digital worlds.
