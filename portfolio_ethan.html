About Ethan

Born in Singapore, Ethan Chen developed a passion for both art and technology from an early age. At just 14, he was creating 3D models as a hobby while studying traditional Chinese painting techniques. This unique blend of disciplines would later define his artistic style.

After earning degrees in both Architecture and Computer Graphics from MIT, Ethan spent years working with leading tech companies before transitioning to full-time digital art in 2018. His work explores how technology can harmonize with nature and human emotion.

"I create environments where the impossible becomes possible," says Ethan. "My goal is to craft spaces that inspire wonder and provoke thought about our future relationship with technology and the natural world."
