About Isabella

Born in Rome to an Italian neuroscientist and an American artist, Isabella Rossi grew up at the intersection of two worlds. Her childhood home was filled with both microscopic brain scans and vibrant canvases, creating a unique perspective on creativity and consciousness.

After earning her PhD in Computational Neuroscience at MIT, Isabella worked for several years on cutting-edge AI research before turning her attention to creative applications. In 2017, she founded Neural Aesthetics Studio, dedicated to exploring the artistic possibilities of artificial intelligence.
